Documentation

1 Getting Bots

To add a bot to your library:

  1. Open the left sidebar
  2. Click "Add Bot"
  3. Choose one of the following options:
    • Upload Image: Provide a character card image
    • Browse Directory: Explore available characters
  4. Click "Add" on any character card to add it to your library

2 Customization

Personalize Kiwi's appearance:

  1. Open the right sidebar
  2. Click "Customize"
  3. Choose your preferences:
    • Theme Color: Pick any color for the app theme
    • Background: Upload a custom image or GIF

3 Generation Settings

Control how the LLM responds to you. Access these settings via right sidebar → Generation:

  • Max Tokens Limits the maximum response length. (1 token ≈ 4 characters / ~1 word)
  • Temperature Controls creativity vs. accuracy. Higher = more creative (risk of gibberish at 1+). Lower = more focused.
  • Top P Adjusts word selection diversity. Higher = more varied vocabulary. Lower = more predictable.
  • Frequency Penalty Reduces repetition. Too high may cause unnatural responses.
  • Presence Penalty Similar to frequency penalty, but increases with repeated word usage.
Tip: Start with default values and adjust gradually to find your preferred balance.

4 LLM Settings

Configure how the AI behaves. Access via right sidebar → LLM Settings:

  • Prompt Input Custom instructions that guide the AI's behavior and speaking style.
  • Preset Selector Quick-toggle between saved prompt configurations for easy switching between different AI personalities.
